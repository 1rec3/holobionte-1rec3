## DETAILED PROFILES

### 1. CLAN GLADIADORES √ó *Hyperolus viridiflavus*
- **Region**: West Africa, Guinea Ecuatorial
- **Specialization**: Territorial behavior, competitive calling
- **Applications**: Sports automation, behavioral prediction
- **Key Trait**: 40+ call types for territorial control
- **IUCN**: Least Concern

### 2. CLAN ALQUIMISTAS √ó *Chiromantis xerampelina*
- **Region**: East & Central Africa
- **Specialization**: Molecular biology, natural polymers
- **Applications**: Biopharmaceuticals, adhesive materials
- **Key Trait**: Foam nest with antimicrobial properties
- **IUCN**: Least Concern

### 3. CLAN ARQUITECTOS √ó *Leptopelis vermiculatus*
- **Region**: Guinea Ecuatorial (Endemic)
- **Specialization**: Bioengineering, habitat design
- **Applications**: Smart ecosystems, assisted breeding
- **Key Trait**: Complex arboreal foam nest construction
- **IUCN**: Vulnerable

### 4. CLAN CAZADORES √ó *Arthroleptis stenodactylus*
- **Region**: Guinea Ecuatorial (Endemic)
- **Specialization**: Sensory systems, predation
- **Applications**: Bio-inspired sensors, AI vision
- **Key Trait**: Advanced visual and chemical detection
- **IUCN**: Not Threatened

### 5. CLAN DRUIDAS √ó *Ptychadena mascareniensis*
- **Region**: Pan-African distribution
