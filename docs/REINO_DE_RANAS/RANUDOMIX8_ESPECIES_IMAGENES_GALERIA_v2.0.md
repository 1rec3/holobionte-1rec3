## 1. CLAN GLADIADORES √ó *Hyperolus viridiflavus*
![Hyperolus](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Hyperolus_viridiflavus.jpg)
- **Common Names**: Painted Reed Frog, Leaf-folding Frog
- **Family**: Hyperoliidae
- **Habitat**: West Africa rainforests, Guinea Ecuatorial
- **Size**: 1.2-1.8 cm
- **Behavior**: Highly territorial, 40+ distinct call types
- **IUCN Status**: Least Concern
- **Tech Application**: Acoustic behavior modeling, AI territorial patterns
- **Reference**: [AmphibiaWeb Hyperolus](https://amphibiaweb.org/)

## 2. CLAN ALQUIMISTAS √ó *Chiromantis xerampelina*
![Chiromantis](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Chiromantis_xerampelina_001.jpg)
- **Common Names**: Foam-nesting Tree Frog, White's Tree Frog
- **Family**: Rhacophoridae
- **Habitat**: East & Central Africa tree canopies
- **Size**: 5-7.5 cm
- **Behavior**: Creates foam nests with biopolymeric properties
- **IUCN Status**: Least Concern
- **Tech Application**: Biopolymer synthesis, natural adhesives
- **Reference**: [IUCN RedList](https://www.iucnredlist.org/)
